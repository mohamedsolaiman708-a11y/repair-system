# Repair Process

## Overview

Every repair order follows a predefined workflow.

The workflow guarantees consistent operations across all branches.

---

## Step 1

Customer arrives.

---

## Step 2

Operator registers customer information.

---

## Step 3

Operator registers device information.

---

## Step 4

Operator selects the responsible technician.

---

## Step 5

Barcode is generated.

---

## Step 6

Receipt is printed.

---

## Step 7

WhatsApp confirmation is sent.

---

## Step 8

Technician repairs the device.

Operator updates the repair status.

---

## Step 9

If customer approval is required:

Operator records the quotation.

Customer approves.

Repair continues.

---

## Step 10

Repair completed.

Customer receives WhatsApp notification.

---

## Step 11

Customer collects the device.

Operator records payment.

Repair order becomes Closed.
