# Barcode Rules

## Purpose

Every repair order receives one unique barcode.

---

## Generation

Automatically generated.

Cannot be edited.

Cannot be duplicated.

---

## Usage

Barcode appears on:

Receipt.

Repair label.

Search.

Delivery process.

---

## Scan Result

Scanning a barcode opens the repair order immediately.

---

## Printing

Barcode should be printable on:

Repair receipt.

Device label.
