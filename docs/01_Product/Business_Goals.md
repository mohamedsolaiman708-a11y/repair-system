# Business Goals

## Main Objective

Help repair centers manage high daily workloads with minimal effort.

---

## Daily Problems

The repair center currently faces:

Paper records.

Lost repair tickets.

Slow customer communication.

Difficulty tracking repair status.

Difficulty monitoring branches.

Manual reporting.

Human errors.

---

## Business Objectives

The system should:

Save employee time.

Increase productivity.

Improve customer satisfaction.

Reduce operational mistakes.

Improve branch visibility.

Provide accurate reporting.

---

## Operational Philosophy

The software should require the fewest possible clicks.

Common tasks should be completed as quickly as possible.

The interface must remain simple even under heavy workloads.
